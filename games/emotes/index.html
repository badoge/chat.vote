How to play - Emote benchmark

1. Streamer adjusts settings then clicks on "Start"

2. Emotes will be shown on screen and chat has to type them in order

3. Viewers that type them correctly move on to the next round

4. Game ends when 1 viewers is left

Streamer can also play by typing in chat
